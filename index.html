Tips & Tricks

Efficient Sorting for Cleaner Loads

Begin by sorting your laundry into separate piles based on color, fabric type, and level of dirtiness. This practice not only prevents color bleeding but also ensures each load gets the right treatment for optimal cleanliness.

The Power of Pre-Treating Stains

Pre-treating stains before washing can make a significant difference in their removal. Use a stain remover or a mixture of water and detergent to treat stains promptly. Allow it to sit for a few minutes before washing for best results.

Maximizing Drying Efficiency

To speed up drying time and save energy, consider using dryer balls or clean tennis balls in the dryer. They help to separate clothes, reduce wrinkles, and improve airflow, resulting in faster and more efficient drying cycles.
